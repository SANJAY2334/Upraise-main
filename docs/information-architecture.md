# UPRISE Information Architecture

## Public Journey

1. Home hero: premium identity, promise, CTA.
2. Who We Are: authority positioning.
3. Founder Spotlight: biography, timeline, philosophy, expertise, achievements, impact.
4. Success Metrics: proof points.
5. Core Services: advisory, media, promotion, campaign strategy, consulting.
6. Companies Worked With: client credibility.
7. Featured Case Studies: narrative portfolio.
8. Testimonials: social proof.
9. Strategic Insights: authority content.
10. Contact CTA: lead capture.

## Navigation

- Home: anchors into the main journey.
- Work: case studies and portfolio.
- Founder: founder profile experience.
- Insights: CMS-backed content.
- Contact: lead capture.

## Admin Modules

- Dashboard
- Founder Management
- Services Management
- Portfolio Management
- Case Studies
- Blog Management
- Testimonials
- Clients
- Media Library
- Lead Management
- SEO Management
- Settings

## User Flow

1. Visitor lands on premium hero.
2. Visitor reviews positioning, founder credibility, services, and case studies.
3. Visitor reads insights or testimonials for authority validation.
4. Visitor submits contact inquiry.
5. API validates CSRF and input, stores contact message, creates lead with `NEW` status.
6. Admin reviews lead, updates status, and audit log records the change.
