# UPRISE Requirements Analysis

## Product Objective

UPRISE is a premium, founder-centric digital platform for event media, brand advisory, promotion, campaign strategy, and strategic consulting. The platform is designed as a story-driven experience rather than a collection of disconnected pages.

## Primary Users

- Prospective clients evaluating UPRISE authority, credibility, and quality.
- Founders and leadership teams seeking advisory, media, or campaign support.
- Internal administrators managing CMS content, media, SEO, testimonials, clients, and leads.

## Experience Requirements

- Premium corporate identity using black, surface black, gold, white, and muted text.
- Playfair Display for headings and Inter for body copy.
- Full-screen hero with premium logo reveal and conversion CTA.
- Founder spotlight as the strongest public section.
- Case studies presented as narratives with challenge, strategy, media, and measurable results.
- Blog and insights architecture for authority building and organic traffic.
- Cookie consent with essential, analytics, and marketing categories.
- Privacy, cookie, and terms policy pages.

## Platform Requirements

- React, TypeScript, Vite, TailwindCSS, Framer Motion, React Router, React Query.
- Node.js, Express, TypeScript API.
- PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM.
- Cloudinary media support for image and video uploads.
- JWT authentication with refresh tokens and role permissions.
- Secure middleware: Helmet, rate limiting, validation, CSRF defense, secure cookies, password hashing, and audit logs.
- Admin dashboard modules for CMS, CRM, media, SEO, settings, and lead management.

## Success Metrics

- Lighthouse targets: Performance 95+, Accessibility 95+, Best Practices 95+, SEO 100.
- Lead capture stored as both contact message and CRM lead.
- Maintainable schema and content architecture for long-term operation.
