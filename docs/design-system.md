# UPRISE Design System

## Brand Tokens

- Background: `#0A0A0A`
- Surface: `#121212`
- Gold Primary: `#D4AF37`
- Gold Accent: `#F4D03F`
- White: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted Text: `#B8B8B8`

## Typography

- Headings: Playfair Display
- Body: Inter

## Interface Principles

- Spacious but efficient.
- Premium, restrained, and executive.
- No Bootstrap-like cards, cheap animations, or color overload.
- Cards are used for repeated items, modules, media, and dashboard surfaces.
- Strong contrast and predictable controls.

## Motion

- Loading animation: black background, logo reveal, gold shine, 1.25 seconds.
- Section motion: subtle fade and rise.
- Interaction motion: minimal, smooth, no bounce-heavy effects.

## Components

- Fixed premium header.
- Public story sections.
- Interactive founder timeline.
- Services segmented panel.
- Narrative case study modules.
- Cookie consent preference manager.
- SaaS-style admin sidebar and dashboard.
